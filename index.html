Health & Nutrition

Why Our Products Work For You

Common nutritional and health advantages across the entire LeanBake range — backed by ingredient science.

💪

High Protein — Every Bite

12–25g of protein per serving across all products. Builds and repairs muscle, reduces hunger hormones, and keeps you fuller for longer than any regular bakery product.

🩸

Low Glycaemic Index

Jowar, almond flour, and oat-based recipes have significantly lower GI than wheat. Slower blood sugar rise means sustained energy and no post-snack crash.

🌾

Rich in Dietary Fibre

2.2–5.6g of fibre per serving — from whole grains, flaxseeds, and psyllium husk. Supports gut health, digestion, and a healthy microbiome.

🚫

Zero Added Sugar

Every LeanBake product contains 0g of added sugar. Where sweetness exists, it comes from Medjool dates, raw honey, or coconut sugar — never refined white sugar.

🧬

No Artificial Anything

No artificial sweeteners (no sucralose, no stevia), no maltodextrin, no artificial colours or flavours. What you read on the label is exactly what's inside.

🫀

Heart-Healthy Fats

Cold-pressed coconut oil, almond butter, and flaxseeds provide healthy mono and polyunsaturated fats. Zero trans fat. Zero hydrogenated oils. Ever.
